The Best dramas according to Ritika Negi

My top three K-dramas of all time

Healer

Best action, triller, mystery i have ever watched. My first and one of the best Ji ChangWook dramas. Everything is just perfect about this drama, from casting to plot. i have watched it twice. Drama that made me fall in love with my Wookie oppa.

Moonlover

Nothing can be better than this cast, as it has my baby Baekhyun in it. The plot is great and has many emotional scenes, and i dont like to cry over sad ending, so I'm still waiting for the season 2 with many fellow fans. It is a beautiful drama.

She was pretty

It is a pretty drama, which made us know about binge watching because you can't just wait to know about what will happen in the next episode. Its just a wholesome drama about love, friendship and the beauty that lies inside.
